A little about me

I am a website designer who enjoys creating clean, organised and beautiful websites.

I have worked in Higher Education for a number of years, most recently teaching students how to use Adobe software and how to build WordPress websites as part of a Publishing & Journalism course. I recently completed a web development course and I've found that coding combines my love of design, problem solving, research and creative thinking. I would love to work with you on your website project and can offer a package tailored to your personal or business needs. When I'm not coding or teaching Adobe software, I'll most likely be found on the Kennet & Avon canal where I live on a narrowboat with my cat, Cassius.
