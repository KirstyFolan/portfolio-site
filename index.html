Hi, I'm a web developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I grew up in Surrey, and currently live on the Kennet & Avon canal on a narrowboat with my cat, Cassius. I have worked in higher education for a number of years, most recently teaching students how to use Adobe software and how to build simple wordpress.com websites as part of a Publishing & Journalism course. Having completed a coding course alongside this role, I've decided to branch out into web development.

When I learned how to build my first website from scratch, I realised I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. It combines my love of design, problem solving and language learning. I’ve now made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organisation and design skills to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer’s PSD, INDD, InVision or XD prototype and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
