A little about me

I am a front end developer who enjoys creating clean, organised and beautiful websites.

I have worked in Higher Education for a number of years, most recently teaching students how to use Adobe software and how to build simple wordpress websites as part of a Publishing & Journalism course. I have learned lots of design and tech skills along the way and also completed a coding course on the side. Having thoroughly enjoyed the course, I've found that coding combines my love of design, problem solving, research and creating. When I'm not coding or teaching Adobe software, I'll most likely be found on the Kennet & Avon canal where I live on a narrowboat with my cat, Cassius.
